
## PRODUCT REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT – SCHOOLTINO

Vision:
AI + CCTV + ERP based fully automated school management system.

Apps:
1. Schooltino – Director/Principal Admin
2. Teachtino – Teacher App
3. Studytino – Student + Parent App

Core Modules:
- User & Role Management
- Academics
- Attendance (Manual + AI)
- CCTV AI Monitoring
- Exams & Results
- Fees & Finance
- Documents & Approvals
- Notifications
- Audit Logs

Key Objective:
Reduce human workload, increase transparency, improve safety & learning.
